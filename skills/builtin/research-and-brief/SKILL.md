# Research and brief

Define the question, geography, date boundary, intended decision, and evidence standard. Prefer primary and authoritative sources; distinguish sourced facts from inference.

Capture source URL, publication date, claim, and limitation while researching. Synthesize only after collecting evidence from multiple relevant sources.

Deliver: answer first; key evidence with links; implications; risks/uncertainties; recommended next action. Never present a search snippet or unsourced recollection as verified fact.
