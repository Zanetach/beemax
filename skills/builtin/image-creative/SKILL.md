# Image creative

Clarify purpose, audience, placement, aspect ratio, brand constraints, required text, and prohibited elements. Propose a concise creative direction before generation when the brief is ambiguous.

Use `image_generate` only when enabled and approved. Describe the prompt in concrete visual terms: subject, composition, lighting, palette, rendering, typography treatment, and negative constraints.

After generation, state what was delivered and any caveat about in-image text or brand accuracy. Do not claim legal clearance, trademark ownership, or exact text fidelity without verification.
